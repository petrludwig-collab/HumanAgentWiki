> "You could never convince a monkey to give you a banana by promising him limitless bananas
> after death in monkey heaven."

**From:** [[Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind]] · **By:** [[Yuval Noah Harari]]

Harari's point: only *Homo sapiens* can believe in shared fictions — and that shared belief is
exactly what lets millions of strangers cooperate.
