> "Everything can be taken from a man but one thing: the last of the human freedoms — to choose
> one's attitude in any given set of circumstances, to choose one's own way."

**From:** [[Man's Search for Meaning]] · **By:** [[Viktor E. Frankl]]

The heart of the book: even when everything else is stripped away, the freedom to choose our
response remains ours. This is the seed of logotherapy.
