---
title: Nothing is as important as you think
category: Quotes
tags: [quote, psychology, focusing-illusion]
---

> "Nothing in life is as important as you think it is, while you are thinking about it."

**From:** [[Thinking, Fast and Slow]] · **By:** [[Daniel Kahneman]]

Kahneman calls this the **focusing illusion**: whatever we attend to looms larger than it really
is. A useful antidote to overrating any single worry or purchase.
