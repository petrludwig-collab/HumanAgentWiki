> "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."

**From:** [[Atomic Habits]] · **By:** [[James Clear]]

Goals set the direction, but day-to-day results come from the systems you build. Want better
outcomes? Improve the system, not just the wish.
