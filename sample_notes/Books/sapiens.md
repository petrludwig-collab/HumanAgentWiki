**Author:** [[Yuval Noah Harari]] · **Published:** 2011 (Hebrew), 2014 (English) · **Signature quote:** [[Limitless bananas in monkey heaven]]

A sweeping history of how *Homo sapiens* came to rule the planet. Harari argues our superpower is
the ability to **cooperate flexibly in large numbers** around shared fictions — money, nations,
religions, companies — things that exist because we collectively believe in them.

## Why it matters
- Frames the **Cognitive Revolution** (~70,000 years ago) as the moment stories let us scale.
- Reframes money, law and nations as **useful shared imagination**.
- A big-picture companion to how minds and societies actually work.
