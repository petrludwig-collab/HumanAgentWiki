**Author:** [[Daniel Kahneman]] · **Published:** 2011 · **Signature quote:** [[Nothing is as important as you think]]

A landmark book on how the mind makes decisions. Kahneman describes two modes of thought:
**System 1** (fast, intuitive, emotional) and **System 2** (slow, deliberate, logical). Many of
our errors come from trusting System 1 where System 2 is needed.

## Why it matters
- Maps the **cognitive biases** that quietly distort judgment — anchoring, availability, loss aversion.
- Built on decades of research with Amos Tversky that reshaped economics and psychology.
- A practical lens for noticing when your gut is fooling you.
