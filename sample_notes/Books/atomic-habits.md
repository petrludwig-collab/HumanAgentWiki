**Author:** [[James Clear]] · **Published:** 2018 · **Signature quote:** [[You fall to the level of your systems]]

A practical playbook for building good habits and breaking bad ones. The core idea: tiny
**1% improvements** compound, and lasting change comes from **systems and identity**, not from
goals or willpower alone.

## Why it matters
- The **Four Laws of Behavior Change**: make it obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying.
- Focus on *who you want to become* (identity-based habits), not just outcomes.
- The most immediately actionable book on this list.

Related: [[Man's Search for Meaning]], [[Thinking, Fast and Slow]].
