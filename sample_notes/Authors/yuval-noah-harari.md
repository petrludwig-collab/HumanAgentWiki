**Wrote:** [[Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind]] · **b. 1976**

Israeli historian and professor at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. He writes big-picture
history that connects biology, anthropology, economics and technology.

His central claim: humans dominate the planet because we can **cooperate flexibly in huge numbers**
by believing in shared fictions — money, nations, religions, rights.
