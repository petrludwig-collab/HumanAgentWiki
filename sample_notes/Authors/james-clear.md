**Wrote:** [[Atomic Habits]] · **b. 1986**

American author and speaker on habits and continuous improvement. His weekly newsletter and the
book *Atomic Habits* (millions of copies sold) turned habit science into a practical system.

His message: you don't need a radical transformation — you need **small, consistent improvements**
and systems that make the good behavior the default.
