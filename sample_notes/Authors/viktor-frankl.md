**Wrote:** [[Man's Search for Meaning]] · **1905–1997**

Austrian psychiatrist and neurologist, and a Holocaust survivor. He founded **logotherapy**, a
form of therapy centered on the human search for meaning as the primary motivational force.

Frankl survived Nazi concentration camps and distilled the experience into a simple, hard-won
insight: we cannot always control our circumstances, but we can always choose our attitude toward them.
